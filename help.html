Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a record?

Use the search bar on the main page. You can search by an individual's name, a business name, or a case number if you have one.

Why can't I find a record I'm looking for?

Some records are filed under a business name rather than an individual's name, particularly for sole proprietorships and DBAs. Try searching the associated business if a name search doesn't return results.

Are these records official?

This portal reflects publicly filed information. For certified copies of any record, contact the County Clerk's office directly.

This is a simulated records portal built for training purposes. No real government records are contained here.
