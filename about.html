About This Portal

The Stearns-Wright County Public Records Portal provides free access to publicly available property records, business license filings, and resolved court records for residents and researchers.

Records displayed here are sourced from county assessor filings, the business licensing office, and public court dockets. Sealed records, juvenile cases, and active investigations are not displayed.

For questions about a specific filing, contact the County Clerk's office during business hours.

This is a simulated records portal built for training purposes. No real government records are contained here.
